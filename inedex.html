CAGLIARI

This website contains all the information you need to know about Cagliari city

Cagliari is an Italian municipality and the capital and largest city of the island of Sardinia, an autonomous region of Italy.It has about 155,000 inhabitants, while its metropolitan city (including Cagliari and 16 other nearby municipalities) has about 420,000 inhabitants. According to Eurostat, the population of the functional urban area, the commuting zone of Cagliari, rises to 476,975. Cagliari is the 26th largest city in Italy and the largest city on the island of Sardinia.
